# Initial Concept
Golang-based web application for personal expense management, focused on a clean monthly workflow and intuitive tracking of recurring and non-recurring costs.

## Vision
To empower individuals with complete awareness and control over their monthly finances through a clear, organized, and intuitive tracking experience.

## Target Audience
The primary users are individuals who want a simple yet effective way to track their personal monthly expenses and gain a better understanding of their spending habits.

## Core Problem
Many people struggle to keep track of their monthly cash flow, often losing sight of which bills are paid and which are still pending. This app provides a central, monthly-focused view to ensure nothing is missed.

## Key Features
- **Monthly Context-First Navigation:** The entire application is structured around a monthly view, allowing users to easily plan and review their finances month-by-month.
*   **Visual Cash Flow Tracking:** Clear visual indicators for paid and unpaid expenses, giving users an immediate sense of their current financial status.
*   **Flexible Category Management:** Organize expenses into logical groups and categories that can be either recurring or specific to a single month.

## User Experience Goals
*   **Visual Clarity:** Present financial data in a way that is easy to scan and understand at a glance.
*   **Intuitive Organization:** Use a logical structure (Groups > Categories > Expenses) that mirrors how people naturally think about their budgets.
*   **Seamless Monthly Transitions:** Make it effortless for users to move between months and carry forward recurring financial commitments.

## Success Metrics
A user is successful when they have achieved total awareness of their monthly expenditures, leading to a more predictable and stress-free financial life.
